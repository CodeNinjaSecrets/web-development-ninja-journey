User Experience

Something we haven't discussed yet, but is crucial for the User Experience (UX) of a website, is its simplicity.

  • Good websites are easy to use and don't overwhelm users with too many choices. Visitors usually have a specific goal in mind, so we should make it effortless for them. Web usability, testing and Responsive Design are key concepts in UX. Reference: Steve Krug's "Don't Make Me Think".
  • Our goal is to make web browsers stay and keep the website captivating because they're just a click away from leaving.
